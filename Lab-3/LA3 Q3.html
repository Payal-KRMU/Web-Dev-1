Nepal

  • Why Cheap: Similar Currency, Affordable food & Accomodation
  • Famous Places: Kathmandu, Pokhara, Mount Everest base camps
  • Budget: ₹1,000-₹2,000/day
  • Visa: Free for Indians

Vietnam

  • Why cheap: Very low cost of living and food
  • Must visit: Hanoi, Halong Bay, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang
  • Budget: ₹2,000-₹3,500/day
  • Visa: E-visa (around ₹1,500)

Indonesia

  • Why cheap: Bali is affordable compared to Western tourist spots
  • Must visit: Ubud, Seminyak, Kuta, rice terraces
  • Budget: ₹3,000-₹4,500/day
  • Visa: Free entry for Indians up to 30 days
